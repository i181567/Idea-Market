"What is IDEA-MARKET? A Brief Overview"

IDEA-MARKET is a marketplace for IDEAS_a place where you can buy and sell IDEAS. It provides an Automated Idea Auctionioning System that facilitates the transfer of ownership of IDEAS. It enables the IDEA seller to get maximum possible monetary value by selling their IDEAS. For a buyer, IDEA-MARKET provides easy access to a bank of great IDEAS that you can purchase.

Each IDEA is stored as a block within our custom-made BlockChain which ensures transparency, security and Immutability. We hope to encourage the birth of great, sustainable and innovative IDEAS that tackle challenging problems in multiple domains by providing a platform that facilitates and benefits the IDEA-creators.

What details do I need to provide for my IDEA proposal?

You will need to provide an Idea Title that is catchy, understandable yet doesnot give your IDEA away. You will also need to provide a 'Brief Description' of your idea along with the problem it is tackling and how your solution IDEA specifically addresses the specified problem.

Moreover, you will provide a list of Problem Domains within which your IDEA lies along with the Tech-Stack(technologies used) that will be needed to possibly implement your IDEA. Lastly, you will provide Viewing Price and Base Ownership Price for your IDEA.

What components of my IDEA will be visible in the MARKET?

The components of your IDEA that will be visible to anyone viewing the MARKET will be your IDEA Ditle, its Problem Domains, Tech-Stack, Viewing Price and Base Ownership Price. All remaining IDEA components will remain hidden.

What is Viewing Price?

It is the price that any visitors exploring the MARKET will pay to view the 'Brief Description' component of your IDEA.

What is Base Ownership Price?

This is the base/initial price point from which the bidding for your IDEA will start.

What is IDEA Approval and Disapproval?

The IDEA proposed by you is matched for its similarity against the IDEAS already stored in the BlockChain to prevent IDEA plagarism/piracy. Your IDEA is Approved and added to our BlockChain if your IDEA brings something new to the existing and stored chain of IDEAS.

If not, your IDEA will be Disapproved and not added to the BlockChain. This also serves he purpose of protecting the rights and trust of our IDEA-creators and sellers.
